My name is Anna. I'm a Graphic Designer and Illustrator.

Originally from Kazakhstan, I now located in Bay Area, California. I am passionate about creating beautiful and astonishing design that improves people's lives. I like to keep design simple and easy to perceive. Being a creative person with unlimited imagination and positive energy, I am able to work in stressful environments with multiple deadlines and always make ideas from good to great. All my work is based on researches and open conversations. My career goals are to continue growing as an artist and inspire other designers. I believe that design makes everything possible.



Projects


Dream

Project Dream is a series of illustrations dedicated to love, dreams and life. Every illustration has an important message for people and encourages them to make the right move. The main goal of this project is to show our society that some invisible and not so meaningful for now things might be very dangerous in the future and we need to pay closely attention to them. Colors, shapes and characters’ positions are carefully chosen in order to allow the viewer to feel and understand the scene from inside. Every single detail brings the illustration to life.


Funny Pug's Life

Funny Pug’s Life is a set of stickers for instant messaging app Telegram. Every sticker is full of energy, friendliness and has its own story. More than half a million users already downloaded the pack and use them every day to express their feelings. My main goal for the set is to help society to communicate with each other and feel more confident with every sent sticker. Pug represents a man’s friend who is always faithful and around. Cartoon style, the body language of the pugs, bright and bold colors make the pack standing and adore people from all over the world.


Hub

Hub is a cryptocurrency project which allows people safely manage their funds. Each illustration in the set reflects the main components of the product such as security, globalization, connection, and availability. The goal of the project is to help users to get a clear understanding of each element and to acknowledge them how to use Hub coin. A mix of bright orange and light grey colors gives to the users the impression of success, determination and the desire to move forward.


Trust Wallet

Trust Wallet is a mobile wallet app that allows its users to send, receive and store cryptocurrency tokens on the Ethereum blockchain. Designed with a focus on simplicity, this open-source wallet aims to provide a platform that is easy and straightforward to set up and use. The set of illustrations reflects the main characteristics of Trust Wallet such as security, decentralize exchange, Dapps and Web3, multi-currency, etc. The goal is to help users to get visual explanation of components of the product and give them the right message. Carefully chosen colors for the illustrations stimulate people’s growth in trust toward the company and also make them feel more confident.
